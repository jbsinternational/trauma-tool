Introductory Video to Module 7.

One Year Later.

Many of the 9 PRTF Waiver States were building capacity in trauma-informed care prior to the start of the waiver. The Demonstration provided the catalyst to crystalize these efforts and focus on the need to increase their work at the system and provider levels to become more trauma-informed. This video contains examples of how 8 of these states increased their efforts and some of the resulting changes over a one year period.
